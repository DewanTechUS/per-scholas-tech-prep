Million Dollar Dip

Photo by Kelsey Hansen Food Stylist: Holly Dreesman Prop Stylist: Addelyn Evans.

Description

Rich and creamy dip (aka Neiman Marcus dip) made with cheese, mayo, bacon, and almonds. Chill before serving with crackers.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup slivered almonds
  • 6 slices bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise
  • 8 oz sharp Cheddar, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • Pinch cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Toast almonds until golden.
  2. Cook bacon until crisp, chop.
  3. Mix mayo with cheddar, then fold in bacon, almonds, onions, and cayenne.
  4. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.

Note

Use a sharp knife for onions to reduce harsh flavors. Serve with sturdy crackers, not thin chips.
