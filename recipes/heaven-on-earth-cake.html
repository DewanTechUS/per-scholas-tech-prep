Heaven on Earth Cake (No‑Bake)

Credit: Hannah Hufham / Food Styling: Amanda Stanfield / Prop Styling: Keoshia McGhee

Description

No‑bake summer dessert: cubes of angel food cake layered with cherry pie filling, pudding cream, and whipped topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 oz) angel food cake, cut into cubes
  • 1 (21 oz) can cherry pie filling, divided
  • 1 (3.4 oz) pkg vanilla instant pudding mix
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk or half‑and‑half
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 (8 oz) container whipped topping, thawed
  • Sliced almonds (optional)

Steps

  1. Layer half of cake cubes in dish, spread with 1 cup cherry filling, then add remaining cake cubes.
  2. Whisk pudding mix with milk and sour cream until smooth; spread over cake.
  3. Top with whipped topping, dot with remaining cherries, swirl, and refrigerate 8+ hours.
